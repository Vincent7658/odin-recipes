Lasagna

Lasagna: The best comfort food.

Lasagna is quite an easy and versitile dish to make. It can be fun to add whatever you like to it to make it your own, but this is my preffered method.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound sweet italian sauage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 whole white onion diced
  • 5 colves of garlic minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounce) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 2 T white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of fresh basil chopped
  • 1 t fennel seeds
  • 1 t gound oregano
  • 1/2 t salt (x2)
  • 1/4 t ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped
  • 1 pund lasagna noodles
  • 30 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 2 T fresh parsley chopped
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/8 t fresh nutmeg ground
  • 1 pound thinly sliced mozzarella cheese about 24 slices
  • 1 cup parmesean cheese freshly grated
